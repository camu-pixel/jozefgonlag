Digital Media Campaigning

For my Digital Media Campaign final year course we were assigned different events to organize by the end of the year. Our group was assigned the "Final Year Showcase event", an event held at the University to give students an opportunity to showcase their University work to industry professionals and employers.

For this event I was in charge of organizing the live stream.

The stream featured:

Streaming to 4 different platforms simultaneously: Twitch.tv, Facebook Live, Twitter(Periscope) and YouTube.

Live on-screen tweets (through TweetAlerts), featuring hashtags and keyword filtering.

Basic stream features such as scrolling text, scene switcher and back-up systems.

The stream was set-up through OBS Studio and Restream.io, a web based service that allows for a singular stream to be streamed to multiple streaming platforms at once.

Furthermore, we created interactive posters for the event which allowed people to scan the flyer and posters through an AR app on their smartphones to load things such as the website and a promotional Youtube video.
